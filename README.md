# Mini Archive

> Every miniature has a story.

Mini Archive is a digital archive and provenance platform for physical miniatures.

Its purpose is to preserve the history, craftsmanship, and ownership of painted miniatures through permanent Archive Records.

Mini Archive is designed for:

- Commission painters
- Display painters
- Competition painters
- Collectors
- Wargamers
- RPG players
- Scale modelers

Mini Archive is game agnostic.

Whether a miniature belongs to Warhammer, Dungeons & Dragons, Marvel Crisis Protocol, Battletech, or another tabletop game, every miniature deserves a permanent digital identity.

---

## Core Features

- Permanent Archive Records
- Unique Archive IDs
- Artist Profiles
- Collection Management
- Progress Galleries
- Paint Recipes
- Competition History
- Campaign Tracking
- Ownership History
- NFC & QR Integration
- Certificates of Authenticity

## Philosophy

Mini Archive is not social media.

Mini Archive is not an online gallery.

Mini Archive exists to preserve the lifetime history of physical miniatures.
